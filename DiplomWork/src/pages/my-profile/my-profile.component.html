Currently application supports two roles: User and Business creator

As user you can search for businesses, see available slots and make appointment for given slot if it is free

As business creator you can create different types of businesses. When you want to create business you have to:

      Give your business name, location and telephone
      Set a picture
      Set business field
      Configure your slots and number of users per slot

Every business has a week plan with slots. When new week begins every user has to propagate slots for next week or edit business
